# Green River Technologies

Green River Technologies is a technology company focused on building reliable, maintainable, and practical technology solutions.

## Mission

Green River Technologies builds reliable, thoughtful software that solves real problems.
Our mission is to design and deliver high-quality technology—from custom software solutions to scalable SaaS platforms—that helps organizations operate more effectively and adapt to an evolving digital world.

## What we do

Our work spans several areas of modern software development, including:

- Custom software and application development
- SaaS product development
- Internal tools and automation systems
- Technology consulting and architecture

## What we believe

Good software should be:

- **Simple** — understandable systems scale better than complex ones
- **Reliable** — systems should behave predictably and be easy to maintain
- **Purposeful** — technology should solve real problems, not create new ones
